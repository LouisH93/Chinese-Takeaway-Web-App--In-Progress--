Welcome

to Golden Bird Chinese

Takeaway

Nestled in the charming village of Llanfairpwllgwyngyll, Golden Bird Chinese Takeaway is a beloved local gem known for its authentic flavors and warm hospitality. Located at 2 Erw Fair, Ffordd Caergybi, our family-run establishment has been serving the community with dedication and passion for over fifteen years.
Here at Golden Bird, we offer a diverse menu that caters to all tastes, featuring classic dishes like sweet and sour chicken, beef chow mein, and vegetable spring rolls. Each dish is prepared with fresh ingredients and traditional recipes, ensuring a delightful culinary experience. Our takeaway is particularly praised for its crispy aromatic duck and flavorful wonton soup, which have become local favorites.
Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction have earned us a loyal following and a 4.0 rating on Tripadvisor.
Whether you're a regular or a first-time visitor, Golden Bird promises a warm welcome and a delicious meal that will leave you coming back for more.
